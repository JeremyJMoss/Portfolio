About Me

Hey, I'm Jeremy and i am 28 years old. I am a self taught Software Developer. I am a Diagnostic Technician by trade looking to change up my career. My hobbies include coffee roasting, bread making and spending time with my wife and my 3 wonderful children.

Background

I had no previous background in coding when i started and am still a service technician at Sutherland Nissan. I started my apprenticeship in 2013 because i didn't know what i wanted to do after school. I got relatively good at diagnostics and troubleshooting and electrical systems and am currently lead diagnostics technician and foreman at Sutherland Nissan on my way to becoming a Master Technician in February of next year if covid lockdowns ever end. I have found that code is constantly evolving and there is always more to learn which has made me want to pursue a career in coding, as the mechanical trade has limited paths to learn. I have been teaching myself to code through udemy courses and youtube videos. I also plan to pursue a computer science degree to help with the transition to a new career.
